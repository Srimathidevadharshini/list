Types of Snakes

  • KING COBRA

    • The king cobra (Ophiophagus hannah) is a venomous snake endemic to Asia. With an average length of 3.18 to 4 m (10.4 to 13.1 ft)



  • ANACONDA

    • The Anaconda is not a poisonous snake species. Anaconda are well known for their extremely powerful coils that can strangle and crush the bones of their prey before being eaten.

    • Python isn't venomous, but they will carry a bunch of potential health issues for humans
